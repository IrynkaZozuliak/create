03.Benefits

All-in-one App for Your Website

With Boost App, you can easily edit and manage your website, regardless of the platform. Move blocks and elements, add new pages, connect new widgets, run tests, and track results. The app is available for Mac and has Android and iOS mobile versions. Boost your website with the Boost App!

Multiple Layouts

Change the look of your site easily with a variety of ready-made templates and beautiful layouts.

Safe Access

Don't worry about security: you are working on a local version of your site, it cannot be hacked or stolen.

Knowledge Base

Refer to it whenever you are stuck. Also, you can connect Confluence and share the documentation with teammates.

Lifetime Updates

Once you've bought the Boost App, you get access to all the updates, whatever device you work on.
